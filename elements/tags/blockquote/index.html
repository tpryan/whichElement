<blockquote>

A blockquote is used to contain a large block of text from another source.

Overview

A <blockquote> is a block quotation from another source. It's not about a quick reference to another source. Rather it's usually bringing in a chunk of content from somewhere else so that your reader can interpret for themselves, rather then just a single sentence.

Conflicting or Confusing Elements

<q>

Where a <blockquote> is a block quotation from another source, a <q> is an inline quote. It's usually just a single sentence or less.
