<summary>

A summation or other information about the content of a <details> element.

Overview

Related Elements

  • <details>

When you have a <details> block, it's nice to have a summary of the content being hidden. Otherwise how would people know they want to click this thing. Oh and yeah, the summary becomes a clicklable toggle to show or hide the content in the <details> element.
