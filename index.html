whichElement?

Trying to answer that age old question:
Should that be a div, a span, or something else.

One of the main challenges I see in building semantic content is picking what tag to use when. This site seeks to help with that. Now, before we get all judgy and preachy let me get a few tenants out there:

  • Perfect semantics are impossible to achieve and judge
  • Sematics exist along a continuum, something can be more or less semantic
  • We encourage semantics, but not at the cost of reasonableless.

Our goal is to suggest things to help you make a good decision about what you should do to be semantic. We will not command you to do things to be absolutely semantic.
